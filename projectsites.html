Project Sites

Embark on a journey through innovation and sustainability at our project sites webpage. Discover the thriving landscapes of our aquafarm, poultry, livestock, feedmill, and realty complexes as we redefine modern agriculture. Join us in cultivating a future where cutting-edge technology meets environmental responsibility, creating a legacy of excellence at every project site we proudly showcase.

Batangas

Nestled in the picturesque coastal province of Batangas, our aquafarm facility harnesses the pristine waters of Batangas Bay for sustainable seafood cultivation, offering a harmonious blend of modern aquaculture practices and scenic landscapes.

Sta Rita

Sta. Rita serves as an ideal location for our poultry farm. Surrounded by the region's lush greenery and agricultural richness, our modernized facility in Sta. Rita epitomizes sustainable broiler and layer production, harmonizing advanced farming practices with the natural beauty of Samar.

Burauen

In the lush landscapes of Burauen, Leyte, our livestock farm thrives amidst rolling hills, providing a haven for pig farming. This serene location complements our commitment to ethical and sustainable livestock practices, ensuring the well-being of our pigs and the environment.

Passi

Positioned in the fertile plains of Passi City, our feedmill operates at the heart of the region's grain-producing hub. Surrounded by vast agricultural fields, Passi serves as an ideal location for crafting premium feeds that contribute to the overall health and productivity of livestock across the region.

Sapian

Our aquafarm in Sapian, Capiz, capitalizes on the pristine waters of the Visayan Sea, combining cutting-edge aquaculture technology with the region's aquatic abundance. Sapian stands as a hub for sustainable aquafarming, producing high-quality seafood while preserving the marine ecosystem.

Puerto Princesa

Amidst the tropical paradise of Puerto Princesa in Palawan, our realty complex stands as a testament to responsible land development. Surrounded by lush greenery and pristine beaches, it offers an idyllic setting for both residential and commercial spaces, harmonizing modern infrastructure with the natural beauty of Palawan.

Ocampo

Nestled in the agricultural heartland of Camarines Sur, Ocampo provides the ideal backdrop for our livestock farm. The expansive fields and verdant landscapes create a conducive environment for ethical and sustainable pig farming practices, ensuring the welfare of our livestock and the surrounding ecosystem.

Calbayog

Situated in the scenic city of Calbayog in Samar, our aquafarm embraces the dynamic waters of the Samar Sea. This location not only ensures sustainable seafood production but also integrates seamlessly with the region's commitment to environmental preservation and responsible aquaculture.

Medellin

Perched along the northern coast of Cebu, Medellin hosts our aquafarm facility, capitalizing on the rich marine resources of the Visayan Sea. This coastal town provides an optimal environment for modernized aquaculture practices, fostering the responsible cultivation of seafood in a region known for its marine biodiversity.
